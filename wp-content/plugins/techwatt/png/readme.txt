| **Featured products**              | `[featured_products]`                                            | `[featured_products limit="4" columns="4"]`     |
| **Sale products**                  | `[sale_products]`                                                | `[sale_products limit="4" columns="4"]`         |
| **Best selling**                   | `[best_selling_products]`                                        | `[best_selling_products limit="4" columns="4"]` |
